Rise of the Ancients - ROR Community Patch Notes

Update 1.4 – July 2024
  • Needlework:
    Cost changed from 50 Food 25 Wood to 50 Food
    (Research Time is unchanged and still 20 sec).
  • House:
    Hit Points increased from 75 to 100
  • Battle Axe:
    Upgrade research time decreased from 50 sec to 45 sec
  • Axeman:
    Train time increased from 25 sec to 26 sec
  • Slinger:
    Cost increased from 40 Food 10 Stone to 45 Food 10 Stone
  • Scout:
    Cost decreased from 90 Food to 85 Food and now have x2 conversion resistance
  • Stone Mining:
    Upgrade cost increased from 100 Food 50 Stone to 120 Food 50 Stone
  • Towers:
    Now cause 100% more damage to Scout Ship-line and Transport Ship-line
    (Affected units are Scout Ship, War Galley, Trireme, Light Transport and Heavy Transport).
  • Composite Bowman:
    Upgrade research time reduced from 100 sec to 80 sec
  • Legionary:
    Movement Speed increased from 1 to 1.05
  • War & Armored Elephant:
    Movement Speed increased from 0.75 to 0.80
  • Heavy Catapult:
    Pierce Armor reduced from 5 to 4
    Blast Radius reduced from 1.5 to 1.4
  • Ballista & Helepolis:
    Projectile Speed increased from 4.067 to 4.9
  • Assyrians (Adjusted Civ Bonuses):
    Archers fire 22/33% faster in Tool/Bronze Age
    (Previously "Archers fire 11/22/33% faster in Tool/Bronze/Iron Age").
  • Greeks (Adjusted Civ Bonuses):
    Academy units move 25% faster and cost -20%
    (Previously "Academy units move 30% faster and cost -20%").
  • Lac Viet (Adjusted Civ Bonuses):
    Military Units created 20% faster starting in Tool Age
    (Previously "Military Units created 20% faster").
  • Yamato (Adjusted Civ Bonuses):
    Scouts cost -10% Cavalry and Horse Archer lines cost -15%
    (Previously "All cavalry units and Horse Archers cost -15%").
Update 1.3 – May 2024
  • Needlework:
    Villagers now gains +10 HP instead of +15 HP.
  • Cavalry, Heavy Cavalry and Cataphract:
    Cost changed from 70 Food 80 Gold to 75 Food 70 Gold
  • Scythe Chariot:
    Blast Damage decreased from 1 to 0.8 (100% to 80%).
  • City Watch:
    Now available for every Civilization.
    Research cost changed from 100 Food 15 sec to 75 Food 20 sec
    (Previously not available for Assyrians, Egyptians and Persians).
  • Urbanization:
    Now available for every Civilization.
    (Previously not available for Choson, Hittites, Lac Viet, Minoans, Phoenicians and Yamato).
  • Zealotry:
    Hit Points bonus decreased from 40 to 30
    (decreased values based on new Needlework values to retain original Zealotry balance).
  • Ballista Tower:
    Reload Time decreased from 3.1 to 3.0
    Projectile Speed increased from 4.067 to 4.9
    (New values are equal to Ballista Tower stats in Aoe1 DE).
  • Lac Viet (Adjusted Civ Bonuses):
    Military Units created 20% faster.
    (Previously "Military Units created 25% faster").
    Archery Range units +1/+2 Armor in Tool/Iron Age, Ballista +2 armor.
    (Previously "Archery Range units and Ballista +2 armor").
  • Macedonians (New TB):
    Archery Ranges work 10% faster
    (Previously "Houses +50 HP").
  • Shang (New TB):
    Government Centers work 80% faster (44% less time)
    (Previously "Town centers provide +4 population room").
  • Bug Fixes:
    Clubman, Axeman and Leather Armor for Infantry now properly display correct names in Capture Age.
Update 1.2 – May 2024
  • No Balance Changes.
  • Fixed a bug where Needlework wouldn’t properly display cost within tech tree or properly update the Dark Green/Light Green visual effect when the technology is researched or not.
Update 1.1 – May 2024 (First Release)
  • Needlework:
    NEW Technology - Tool Age - Town Center
    Villagers +15 HP and +1 Attack
    Cost: 50 Food 25 Wood, Research Time: 20 sec
    (Available to every Civilization except Sumerians).
  • Clubman:
    Train time increased from 26 sec to 30 sec
  • Axeman:
    Train time decreased from 26 sec to 25 sec
  • Battle Axe Upgrade:
    Research time increased from 40 sec to 50 sec
  • Leather Infantry Armor:
    Research time increased from 30 sec to 45 sec
  • Bowman:
    Projectile Speed decreased from 8 to 7
    (only affects Bowman).
  • Zealotry:
    Hit Points bonus decreased from 40 to 25
    Melee attack bonus damage decreased from 7 to 6
    (decreased values based on Needlework values to retain original Zealotry balance).
  • Sumerians (New Civ Bonus):
    Villagers have +1 Attack in Tool Age.
    (Other Civ Bonuses unchanged, do not have access to Needlework Technology).
